Welcome to First Vault

This is currently a work in progress.

Goal

If you are familiar with Dungeons and Dragons or Pathfinder, you have probably spent time looking over various tables trying to find the right equipment for your characters. Many online resources exist, such as d20pfsrd, but the information they present is mostly static. First Vault aims to be a comprehensive and interactive resource that makes it easy to find the items you want.
